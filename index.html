How It Works

Our hybrid approach combines the best of AI and human expertise to deliver exceptional hiring results.

1

Craft the Perfect Job Post

Use our AI model tailored for HR to generate an attractive, inclusive, and role-specific job description.

2

Automated Talent Sourcing

SelectraAI scrapes and analyzes thousands of profiles across platforms to build a high-quality candidate pool.

3

Smart Shortlisting

Our AI engine filters candidates based on skills, experience, and fit. Optionally, our HR experts refine the list.

4

Candidate Outreach

Automatically initiate contact with top candidates using personalized AI-generated messages. Human review optional.

5

Engagement & Interest Validation

Receive a shortlist of candidates who are not only qualified but also interested and available for interviews.
